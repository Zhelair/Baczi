## Zi_Chou_combine_earth
tags: Zi, Chou, combine, earthly_branch, Water, Earth, wealth
school: classical
confidence: high

Zi (Rat) and Chou (Ox) form a six-combination that transforms to Earth when Earth is the dominant element. This combination neutralizes the Zi-Wu clash — if the natal chart has a Wu-Zi clash and a Chou arrives in a luck or annual cycle, it can "rescue" the Zi and stabilize the relationship palace. Zi-Chou combination generally brings financial consolidation and property gains during its active period. However, if the transformation to Earth does not complete (insufficient Earth dominance), the two branches remain in partial combination without transformation.
